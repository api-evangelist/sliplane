Five alternatives to Browserless for headless browser automation, comparing Browserbase, Hyperbrowser, Steel, ScrapingBee, and Selenium Grid by features, pricing, and use cases. Browserless is a headless Chrome browser as a service handling web scraping, screenshots, PDFs, and automated testing via REST API, with paid plans from $25-$350/month.
